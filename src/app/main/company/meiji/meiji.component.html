Meiji Holdings Company, Ltd is a Japanese holding company that was established on April 1, 2009, after the stock transfer from Meiji Seika and Meiji Dairies. It is the 4th largest confectionery company in the world

Meiji's Group priorities

  • R&D - In order to grow, Meiji's aim is to satisfy evolving consumer tastes and meet the challenges of a diverse range os societal requirements.
  • Quality - Trust is built on maintaining a sterling reputation over generations
