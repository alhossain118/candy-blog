Mondelez International is one of the world's largest snack companies with global net revenues of $25.9 billion and net earnings of $3.4 billion in 2018. They sell monstly snacks including biscuits, chocolate, gum, and candy as well as various cheese and grocery and powdered beverage products.

Mondelez's mission is to empower people to snack right. They aim to provide the right snack at the right time, made the right way. They produce high-quality snacks made with sustainable ingredients that consumers can feel good about.
